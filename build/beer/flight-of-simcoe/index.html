Flight of Simcoe

A bitter yet balanced IPA that is aromatically pleasing due to the heavy amount of dry-hopping with Falconer’s Flight and Simcoe. The Falconer’s Flight provides the slight citrus aroma while the Simcoe compliments it with strong pine aroma.

ABV 6.3%
IBU 70
Malts 2-Row, Vienna, Caramel 40L, Carapils
Hops Galena, Cascade, Chinook, Centennial, Falconer's Flight, Simcoe, Pacifica

| IPA | 6.3% Alcohol By Volume
